Who are we?

We are team sprints and giggles! We are a combination of graduates which collaborated together in an agile manner to create a website to demonstrate what we have learned throughout the course of the bootcamp. We have also aimed to demonstrate such knowledge by applying it to the project overall and the website development.

Our Courses

Agile Fundamentals

Course Description - The agile course aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the agile approach, agile manifesto, agile frameworks and agile processes. The course is recommended for anyone wanting to know about agile and wanting to gain a basic understanding of agile with no pre-existing knowledge on the agile topic. There are multiple choice tests at the end of each section to serve as an indicator of how well you understand agile.

Business Analysis

Course Description - This course aims to provide an overview of business analysis, stakeholder analysis, requirement analysis as well as a terms of reference. This course is recommended for anyone wanting to develop an understanding of the role of business analyst within an organisation. There is a multiple choice test available at the end of the course to assess your understanding of the course.
