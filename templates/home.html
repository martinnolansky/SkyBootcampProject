Who are we?

We are team Sprints and Giggles! We are a combination of graduates which collaborate in an agile manner to create a website that demonstrates what we have learned throughout the course of the bootcamp. We also aimed to demonstrate such knowledge by applying it to the project overall and the website development.

Our Courses

Agile Fundamentals

The agile course aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the agile approach, manifesto, frameworks and processes. The course is recommended for anyone wanting to know about agile and wanting to gain a basic understanding of its processes with no pre-existing knowledge of the agile topic.

Business Analysis

This course aims to provide an overview of business, stakeholder, requirement analysis as well as a terms of reference. This course is recommended for anyone wanting to develop an understanding of the role of the business analyst within an organisation. There is a multiple choice test available at the end of the course to assess your understanding of the course.
